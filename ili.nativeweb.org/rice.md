# Comment: _Rice v. Cayetano_

### By Steven T. Newcomb, Director of the Indigenous Law Institute

March 13, 2000

In 1988, the U.S. Justice Department issued an advisory memorandum to the Department of State, in which the unconstitutional and illegal nature of the 1898 Newlands Resolution is quite clearly spelled out.

The Justice Department memo enables us to arrive at a number of conclusions highly disturbing to the status quo. No annexation of the Hawaiian Islands ever legally occurred in 1898.
