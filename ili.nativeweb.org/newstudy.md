# New Studies by Native American Scholar Document the Role of Christianity in Historic Oppression

### by Fred Whitehead

Last autumn I read in the Mid-October issue of the paper News from Indian Country, a lead article by Valerie Taliman, "Delegation wants Vatican to revoke the Cetera Bull."

Steven Newcomb, a Shawnee/Lenape legal scholar stated: "Indian people are denied their rights in United States laws simply because they were not Christians at the time of European arrival."
