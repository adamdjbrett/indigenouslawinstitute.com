[Listen to Episode 7: A Quantum View of Free Existence as Entangled Indeterminacy on *Domination Chronicles*.](https://dominationchronicles.com/episodes/e007-quantum-view/)

Newcomb and d'Errico explore how quantum theory can help rethink free existence, relationality, time, and domination.

ILI's work challenges the assumption that domination is the only possible structure of reality. Episode 7 opens that question through quantum ideas such as indeterminacy and entanglement, using them to rethink free existence beyond imposed sovereignty and command.

This short resource post is part of an Indigenous Law Institute guide to the *Domination Chronicles* archive. The goal is to help readers move from ILI's work on empire, Christian discovery, federal Indian law, and the right of domination into the podcast conversations where those themes are examined episode by episode.
