[Listen to Episode 8: Words & Meanings on *Domination Chronicles*.](https://dominationchronicles.com/episodes/e008-words-meanings/)

This episode examines how everyday words hide deeper systems of domination and shape understandings of power.

This is one of the clearest episodes for readers who want to understand why ILI keeps returning to language. Newcomb and d'Errico show how words can make domination appear reasonable, legal, or inevitable unless their meanings are carefully unpacked.

This short resource post is part of an Indigenous Law Institute guide to the *Domination Chronicles* archive. The goal is to help readers move from ILI's work on empire, Christian discovery, federal Indian law, and the right of domination into the podcast conversations where those themes are examined episode by episode.
