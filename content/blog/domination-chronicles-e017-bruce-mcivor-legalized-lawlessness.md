Bruce McIvor joins the hosts to discuss how Canada's legal system is used as a tool of ongoing colonization and domination.

Bruce McIvor's phrase "legalized lawlessness" gives ILI readers a sharp way to hear how colonial states manipulate legality. The episode focuses on Canada while illuminating patterns that also matter across federal Indian law and Indigenous rights struggles.

This short resource post is part of an Indigenous Law Institute guide to the *Domination Chronicles* archive. The goal is to help readers move from ILI's work on empire, Christian discovery, federal Indian law, and the right of domination into the podcast conversations where those themes are examined episode by episode.
