[Listen to Episode 3: Symbols, Enigmas, Curiosity on *Domination Chronicles*.](https://dominationchronicles.com/episodes/e003-symbols/)

This episode explores statues, seals, emblems, and other symbols as tools in the creation of a domination reality.

ILI's work has always treated language and culture as part of law's operating system. Episode 3 extends that attention to symbols, asking how public images, seals, statues, and emblems train people to accept a domination reality as ordinary.

This short resource post is part of an Indigenous Law Institute guide to the *Domination Chronicles* archive. The goal is to help readers move from ILI's work on empire, Christian discovery, federal Indian law, and the right of domination into the podcast conversations where those themes are examined episode by episode.
