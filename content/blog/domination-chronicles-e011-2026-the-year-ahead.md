A New Year conversation on domination, free existence, law, language, technology, and urgent questions for 2026.

This episode offers a broad orientation to the work ahead. For ILI readers, its value is in connecting old structures of domination with contemporary questions about law, language, technology, and the future of free existence.

This short resource post is part of an Indigenous Law Institute guide to the *Domination Chronicles* archive. The goal is to help readers move from ILI's work on empire, Christian discovery, federal Indian law, and the right of domination into the podcast conversations where those themes are examined episode by episode.
